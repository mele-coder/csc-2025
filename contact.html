Effective Strategies

Pollution is a serious issue worldwide which is caused by us leading to consequences not only to human health but wildlife and nature. In the video it goes over ways to help reduce air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution. Being able to use these strategies will help make the world a better place. Through this we are able to promote kaitiakitanga by helping protect the environment from harm and being a guardian. It is everyone's responsibility to help prevent pollution from getting worse in the future, we should work together to ensure a sustainable future for everyone. A cleaner environment means a safer and healthier environment not only for humans but wildlife as well. Stand together to make a difference in the world.
